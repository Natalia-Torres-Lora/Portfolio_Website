About Me

Hola! I am a curious, creative, and always learning software developer from Puerto Rico. Being bilingual in English and Spanish, I love connecting with diverse teams and communities. My tech journey has taken several twists and turns, and each challenge has helped me grow both technically and personally. I fell in love with programming because of its power to solve real problems and transform ideas into reality. I learn best by building projects, troubleshooting bugs, and exploring new technologies hands on; whether diving into Python, experimenting with front-end tools, or picking up something entirely new. Perseverance, creativity, and collaboration are at the heart of my work. I believe technology should be accessible, inclusive, and open to fresh ideas. I’m excited to join a mission-driven team where I can contribute, learn, and make a positive impact, especially in junior backend or full-stack roles.
